About Galants & Legends

Botswana's premier quantity surveying and construction advisory firm with a proven track record of excellence.

Our Story

Galants & Legends was established in Botswana with a vision to provide exceptional quantity surveying services to the construction industry. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the industry.

Our journey began with small residential projects, and today we handle complex commercial and government infrastructure projects worth hundreds of millions of Botswana Pula.

With our headquarters in Gaborone and offices across Botswana, we are strategically positioned to serve clients throughout the country and beyond.
