Bill of Quantities (BOQ)

Our Bill of Quantities service provides a detailed breakdown of all materials, labor, and costs required for your construction project. This essential document serves as the foundation for accurate budgeting and cost control.

What We Provide:

  • Detailed measurement and quantification of all construction elements
  • Accurate cost estimates based on current market rates
  • Comprehensive documentation for tender purposes
  • Regular updates and revisions as project requirements evolve
  • Expert advice on cost optimization opportunities

Our BOQ services have helped clients save up to 15% on project costs through accurate quantification and pricing.

Quantity Surveying Consultancy

Our Consultancy Includes:
